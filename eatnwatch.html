Eat 'n Watch 'em Crash!

Hydrous Wakeparks is a cable wakeboarding training facility, focused on producing the best wakeboarders in the area! The unique overhead cable system is used to pull the riders around a 10-acre lake. This style of system allows for a cheaper and hassle-free way for wakeboarders to enjoy a great ride, the alternative being needing access to a wakeboarding boat and having an experienced wakeboarding boat driver. Each park is accommodated with multiple beginner training lakes designed specifically to teach and help aid first time riders in the process of getting up and riding the wakeboard!


The Hydrous cable park surrounds the Towers Tap House patio making it the perfect place for parents and spectators to sit back and enjoy all the action in a very laidback environment! Towers Tap House is known for its great food, extensive selection of draft beers and amazing views of the cablepark.


The exclusivity of Hydrous is what makes us unique! There are only 36 cable parks currently in the U.S., and we have one in our backyard for our guests to enjoy.
