Summer Camp

Available at both Little Elm and Allen Station locations!
Hydrous Summer Camps are the perfect way to get your kids outdoors and active during the summer. What could be better than spending a week on the water learning how to wakeboard?!


Morning Camps Full Week: $225

Our Full Week Morning Camps are Monday-Friday / 8:30am – 11:30pm & are for ages 8 – 15. Campers will have full access to the park including both beginner and main cable systems. During our Morning Camps the park is closed to the public and ran at slower speeds making it great for first time riders and beginners. Hydrous’ Full Week Morning Camps max out at 25 participants so be sure to book early!

Book Now!



Afternoon Camps Full Week: $225

Hydrous’ afternoon camps are great for riders that have already mastered the beginner training lakes! Our afternoon camps are Main Cable Only and riders will NOT have access to the beginner training systems. Hydrous is open to the public during our afternoon camps and is limited to 15 participants. Our afternoon camps are Monday – Friday / 1pm-4pm & are for ages 8 – 15.

Book Now!



All Day Camps Full Week: $450

Hydrous’ Full Day camps are great for riders that have already mastered the beginner training lakes! Our Full Day camps are Main Cable Only and riders will NOT have access to the beginner training systems in the afternoon. Hydrous is open to the public during the afternoon portion and is limited to 8 participants. Our Full Day camps are Monday – Friday / 1pm-4pm & are for ages 8 – 15. Lunch is NOT provided for Full Day campers. Participants must bring their own lunch.

Book Now!



Single Morning Session: $50

Hydrous offers Single Morning Camp Sessions for those who are not able to attend a Full Week Session. Our Single Session Morning Camps are only available Wednesday, Thursday, & Friday / 8:30am – 11:30pm & are for ages 8 – 15. Campers will have full access to the park including both beginner and main cable systems. During our morning camps the park is closed to the public and ran at slower speeds making it great for first time riders and beginners. Hydrous’ single session Morning Camps max out at only 5 participants so be sure to book early!

Book Now!



Saturday Morning Kids Camps: $40

Our Saturday Morning Kids Camps are single session and are perfect for the weekend warriors! Our Saturday Morning Camps are every Saturday starting the first weekend in May and run all the way through September. Camps are from 9:30am – 11:30am & are for ages 8 – 15. The park is closed to the public during this time and is ran at slower speeds making it perfect for first time riders & beginners! Participants have full access to the park and can use both the beginner training lakes and the main cable system. Hydrous Saturday Morning Kids Camps max out at 25 participants so be sure to book early!

Book Now!
