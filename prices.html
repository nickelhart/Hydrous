Gift Cards

Hydrous Gift Cards make a great present for anyone interested in, or currently wakeboarding at Hydrous Wakeparks. You can purchase a Gift Card online and have it emailed to either you or the person you are purchasing the card for. Choose one of our suggested dollar amounts or select Custom Amount and input any whole dollar amount for $25 or more. Hydrous Gift Cards can be used towards Main Cable Passes, Rentals, Hydrous Retail, and much more.
