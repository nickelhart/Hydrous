Hydrous Tube Fest

Join us for the 4th of July!

Bring your family out to Hydrous for the 4th of July for our celebration! The night will begin with the Pro Wakeboard Demo at 7:00pm. We'll have an hour of tubing that begins at 7:30pm. Life jackets are required and will be available for rent for $5. Tubes will also be available for rent for $5 each. Riders can bring their own life jackets (if the are coast guard approved) and tubes if they wish. Fireworks will begin at 9:00pm for viewers on the shores of Hydrous. Live music will be playing all night at the Towers Tap House.

Show us your spirit and dress up in your best 4th of July outfits. Prizes will be given to the most patriotic people we see!
