Live Music

Towers Tap House strives to create a very laid back and relaxed laketime vibe. To help maintain this atmosphere, we invite one of our many local bands to play on our stages every Friday and Saturday and for other specal events we host.


Our musical performers play a range of genres, including country, classic rock, reggae, and indie, helping to guaruntee that our guests get to listen to music they enjoy as they eat.


If you or someone you know would like the opportunity to perform on one of our stages, please contact us via email at info@towerstaphouse.com.
